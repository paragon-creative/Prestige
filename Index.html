SHOOTING MACHINE

Improve Daily

Most players find it hard to get enough shots up simply because they do not want to chase the ball down after they bricked it halfway across the gym. With our new shooting machine, you will be able to maximize your training and take your game to the next level.

Wider Rebounding Net

Most players struggle to get shots up on other machines because their basketballs get jammed. Others have trouble because their basketballs bounce outside the net. Our new shooting machines solves both of those problems with a wider rebounding net. Our net is 4.5 inches wider and 9 inches taller. This helps players shoot with the proper arc needed for the perfect and helps reduce the number of jams.

Improved Tracking System

Shooting machines have been pretty limited in terms of tracking shots. Not anymore. With our new machine, you will be able to track your total shots, made shots, percentage of made shots, and percentage of each spot on the floor. This machine was built to help players to take their game to the next level.
